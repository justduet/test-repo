Customer Service
We're family people, just like many of our customers. And it's our family values that inspire us to provide you with customer service from a team of people who really care. So, if you want to place an order, discuss a query or if things don't work out as expected (we're human and that sometimes happens!), we're here to help. Simply use the contact details provided on this page. We also have web chat available during our opening hours if that's more your thing! Our current online hours are as follows: Monday to Friday: 9am to 5pm. Hours may be extended during Christmas and Mother's Day. It might also be useful for you to know: We aim to respond to any customer contact within one working day.
